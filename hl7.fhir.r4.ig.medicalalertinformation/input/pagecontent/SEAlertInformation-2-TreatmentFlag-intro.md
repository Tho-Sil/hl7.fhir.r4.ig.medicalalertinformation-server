# Treatment – Introduction

This profile is used to document and highlight ongoing or previous treatments that are of particular importance for the patient's care and safety. Examples include medication treatments, dialysis, anticoagulant therapy, or other treatments that require special attention during care interventions.

The purpose is to ensure that important information about the patient's treatments is easily accessible and can be considered in all care situations. The selection of treatments is based on the Swedish National Board of Health and Welfare's specification
